My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Queen of the Damned

Vampire movies are my favourite movies of all time. Not only is this the best vampire movie to exsist but it also comes across as a love story with amazing music.

Tarot

It's all about them horror movies! Probably the best recent horror movie I've seen in a really long time.

10 Things I Hate About You

The title says it all. A beautifully romantic movie that never ages.
